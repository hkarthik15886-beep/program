POP CULTURE

Pop culture, short for popular culture, is the collection of mainstream practices, beliefs, trends, and products (like music, movies, fashion, tech, slang) that are dominant and widely accessible in a society at a given time, driven by mass media, consumerism, and social interaction, serving as a shared cultural language and a reflection of societal changes. It contrasts with high culture, being accessible to everyone, constantly evolving, and shaped by both creators and passionate fan communities.

About how podcast content is stored

The podcast content will be stored digitally, primarily as audio files in formats like MP3 or AAC [1]. These files are generally saved on secure, robust cloud storage services (such as Amazon S3, Google Cloud Storage, or Microsoft Azure), which offer high reliability, scalability, and data redundancy to prevent loss [1].
