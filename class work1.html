LED Stripe Light

LED stripe lights are flexible lighting strips embedded with small LED chips that provide bright, energy-efficient illumination. They are easy to install and can be cut to fit different lengths, making them ideal for both decorative and functional lighting. Commonly used under cabinets, along ceilings, or behind furniture, LED stripe lights offer uniform light output, long lifespan, low heat generation, and are available in various colors and brightness levels to suit different spaces and moods.

USES OF LED STRIPE LIGHTS AND PRICE DETAILS

  • Accent Lighting: Used to highlight architectural features, artwork, or furniture.
    Price: $15 - $30 per 16.4 feet (5 meters).
  • Task Lighting: Installed under cabinets or shelves to provide focused lighting for workspaces.
    Price: $20 - $40 per 16.4 feet (5 meters).
  • Backlighting: Placed behind TVs or monitors to reduce eye strain and enhance viewing experience.
    Price: $25 - $50 per 16.4 feet (5 meters).
  • Decorative Lighting: Used for parties, holidays, or ambiance creation in living spaces.
    Price: $10 - $25 per 16.4 feet (5 meters).
  • Outdoor Lighting: Weatherproof LED strips for patios, gardens, or pathways.
    Price: $30 - $60 per 16.4 feet (5 meters).
